Top Features

Locally supported Businesses

Now avail the benefit of having all your local shops/stores/home services/ service providers at your fingertips! Select and purchase from their products and services range

Omni Channel- Secured Chat App

We are the only platform which allows customers to directly chat with all Vendors with end-to-end encryption to provide privacy. Manage and track your orders within a chat interface as well.

Sole App for both Customers and Businesses

Our platform is for everyone! From Customers to Businesses and Delivery Partners, All can avail of the benefits and order on the app. Abundant User Engagement.

Shared devices and management for efficiency

A single user can access their account on multiple devices ensuring uniformity and ease in accessing the platform. Continue shopping and chatting regardless of your device!

Bulk uploads of Products and access to our library

Our built-in MIHY library consists of variety of products and services from 180+ types of businesses. The customers have a choice to best fit their needs.

Ad boards, Not ads

Businesses can efficiently offer deals/discounts without irritating the customers, through our Ad boards. Say goodbye to constant ad pop-ups and notifications. Customers will never miss a deal from their favourite stores!

No Catalogue Required

Forget the hassle of a catalogue as you can create raw orders with only Text, Photos, Audio and Video. Confirm your bill and place an order!

Create and manage your Shopping list

Design and access your Shopping list by comparing your requirements to products and services from other businesses and even access quotations.

Smart Order Tracking

Our smart E2E Order tracking with Merchants, Services and CSDs, covers everything from order placement to delivery. Manage your payments and refunds within chats.

Delivery Partners & Crowd Sourcing Delivery

Our Built-in Delivery Management System accounts for exceptional online delivery experiences for Home deliveries and Store pickups.

Easy and Secure Payments with immediate Settlements & Refunds*

All types of payments are supported on our platform and are free. The Payments are automated to warrant quick settlements on both sides.

Support for B2C and B2B - Wholesale and Retail business

Businesses can offer wholesale prices to other businesses online. A Credit (Khata) account can be availed by trusted customers.

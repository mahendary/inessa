For Customers

  • A wide range of access to vendors which ensures that you can choose the most suitable one. Additionally, prices are sustainable and pocket-friendly!
  • Enhanced Service levels and quicker delivery times due to sustainable relations and on-boarding of hyper-local retailers.
  • Fine customer experience with an enhanced scope for innovation.
  • Chat App - Users can freely chat with service providers (Merchants, Service Providers, Delivery Persons and Contacts) on the app.
  • Efficient - All stores, services, and delivery options near your location are now available at your fingertips.
  • Diverse Shopping Experience - You can Place an Order in multiple ways like:
    • Browse Catalogue
    • Shopping List
    • Raw Order Item by simply typing out what you want.
    • Photos or Audio.
    • Adboards.
    • A simple message with your requirements can be typed and directly sent to the Merchant.
  • Price Comparison - Search Products and get to see the range of prices from Different Merchants which gives you the benefits to compare and buy the best!
  • Order Tracking – You have access to a complete E2E Order Tracking and an easy and quick cancel/return policy in case you are dissatisfied with your order.
  • Delivery Partner – You can assign yourself a trusted person as a delivery partner to pick up your order from the store.
  • Multiple Payment Options and ‘Khata’ – The app provides you with all payment options with Razor pay integration and ‘Khata’ for regular users.
  • Crowd Sourcing Delivery - You can place an Order with CSD to pick up and drop items from Source to Destination.
  • Report Business – Feel dubious about a local business? Fear not as an Option to Report a Business Profile is provided to you in case of Authenticity issues.
